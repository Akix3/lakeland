Japanese Traditional Culture

Photo credit : Kanazawa City

    In the Edo period, Kanazawa was the capital of Kaga Domain. The Kaga Domain was the biggest domain in the period expect Tokugawa Shogunate, and Kanazawa was the 4th largest city in Japan. (1. Edo, 2.Osaka, 3.Kyoto, 4.Kanazawa, 5.Nagoya)
    However, Kaga Domain wasn’t originally compatible with Tokugawa Shogunate before the Edo period, and they needed to avoid to be seen as dangerous from Tokugawa. So, they didn’t use ample funds for military, in contrast, they used it for art and culture. For example, the scale of Kanazawa castle is not big considering the scale of Kaga Domain, but the castle is very beautiful and interesting. The variety of Ishigaki, stone wall, is most numerous in Japanese castles, and they are not only for defense, but also for art.
    Please enjoy Kanazawa luxuriant traditional culture!

Kanazawa Castle & Kenrokuen Garden

    Kanazawa castle was the headquarters of Kaga Domain. There are many traditional architectures of Japanese castle. You can feel the majesty of Kaga Domain.
    Kenrokuen garden was the garden for a daimyo (feudal lord) of Kaga Domain. This garden is one of the 3 greatest garden in Japan. (The others are Kairakuen garden and Korakuen garden.)

Cyaya Street

    In the Edo era, chaya street was the entertainment district. In chaya, people can enjoy “Ozashiki-asobi” and the performance of dance and song by geisha.
    In this area, there are beautiful Japanese traditional buildings, and this area is one of the most popular photo spot. In addition, there are many Japanese sweets. You must be fun!
