Ohmicho Market

History

Photo credit : Kanazawa City

    In Kanazawa during the Edo era, there are two big fish markets before the formation of Ohmicho market. However, in 1690, these market were badly damaged by a great fire. After that, these market are combined into one at Ohmicho. This is the origin of Ohmicho Market. The first description of this market in history books written by Kaga Domain is found in 1722.
    For almost 300 years, Ohmicho market function as “Kanazawa citizens’ kitchen pantry”. The long history of this market is made the energetic but also warm atmosphere.

How To Enjoy

Photo credit : Kanazawa City

    There are about 200 shops of fish, meat, vegetable, and fruit in Ohmicho market. You can enjoy fresh and delicious Kanazawa food. The food you must try in the market is Sashimi don. Sashimi don is a bowl of rice with sashimi on top. This is the most popular Kanazawa food for tourists. It’s because sashimi in Kanazawa is most delicious sashimi in Japan! You must try it. However, there are too many people who want to eat it in lunch time and you need to wait a long time. So, I recommend you to try to eat it in morning!
