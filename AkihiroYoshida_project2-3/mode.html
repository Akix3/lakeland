Modern Culture

    During the WWII, most of big cities in Japan had suffered the ravages of the war by air raid. Many cities were destroyed by fire, however Kanazawa didn’t get the attack. So, there are a lot of scene of Japan which haven’t changed the old days, and the culture has been accumulated for a long period. This is one of the most biggest attractions of Kanazawa. You can feel many culture in many era in Kanazawa.
    In contrast, Kanazawa is also rapidly developing. There are a lot of new culture in the city with previous culture. The collaboration is rarely seen in Japan. The representative spot of new culture in Kanazawa is 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art.

Ohmicho Market

Ohmicho Market is called “Kanazawa citizens’ kitchen pantry”. There are about 200 shops of fish, meat, vegetable, and fruit. The origin of Ohmicho Market is the fish market started in 1722. Almost 300 years, this market is used by people in Kanazawa as their kitchen pantry. You can enjoy fresh and delicious Kanazawa food.

21 Century Museum

21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art is representative of the new culture in Kanazawa. People in Kanazawa called the museum “Marubi” which means a round museum because of the shape of the building. This is the most popular sightseeing spot for young people. In 2015, this museum was at the top of the ranking of number of visitors in museums in Japan.
