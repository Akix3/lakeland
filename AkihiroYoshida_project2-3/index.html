    Kanazawa is the capital city of Ishikawa prefecture which is located in the Sea of Japan side. This city had a population of 465,699(2020). It takes only two and a half hours to go from Tokyo to Kanazawa, using Hokuriku Shinkansen which was started in 2015. Kanazawa is designated as Core City, and it is the metropolis of Hokuriku area.
    The characteristic of Kanazawa is that it is sightseeing city. The reason why Kanazawa grow as a sightseeing city is it is one of the most artistic city in Japan. Over the years, people in Kanazawa have valued the art spirit, and accumulated the culture of Kanazawa. The culture can be devided into 3 by the era. First is Japanese traditional culture in the Edo Era, second is westernized culture from the Meiji era to the Showa era, and third is modern culture from Heisei era until now. Let's see the most artistic city, Kanazawa!
