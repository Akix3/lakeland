The Beginning Part

Here we have two images. Try floating both of them. You will find that it is very difficult to place them exactly the way you want to. You can fix this in two ways: (1) by using a "clear" with the second image, or (2) by putting both images inside a special block tag, and then float the block, not the images. Use the <figure> tag, because it is created specially for images.
