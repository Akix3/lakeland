The Beginning Part

This time, use position: absolute; and then notice that the text below the image pops upward. Why? Because the absolute positioning "raises" the object out of the normal flow. Once you do that, you may position the object anywhere on the page, using the top, right, bottom, and left properties. Notice that the position is relative to the edge of the browser page. Notice that the object then scrolls normally from that position.
